About Churricos

Churricos offers a different variety of churros at events and festivals all over the Greater Toronto Area. We utilize only the best ingredients to prepare our churros because we specialize in festival desserts. At Churricos we prepare churros with only the highest quality and fresh ingredients using traditional techniques.

The joy of every festival-goer is our utmost priority, that is why plain, flavored filled, and fresh made churros are offered to all. We promise a delightful churro experience every time.

Serving the Greater Toronto Area since 1999
